1993 Land Rover Defender 110 SUV

Thanks to the 25-year rule on importing foreign cars into the US, classic early- to mid-90s Land Rover Defenders are starting to pop up for sale with increasing regularity. This 1993 Defender 110 is one of the best-looking we've seen and looks to be a steal at its buy-it-now price on eBay. The list of work done is extensive, from bodywork to nearly every gasket on the body and drivetrain being replaced and a new quilt-stitched leather interior that is a work of art. The engine was replaced with a newer Land Rover 200 Tdi diesel and restored T77 transmission, keeping the restoration Land Rover-centric. The exterior was repainted in the original Cosmo Black, and accessories like a roof rack, genuine Land Rover side steps and checker plate, and a huge dock rope scream "overlanding". Buy it now and it includes your choice of an air conditioning unit or free shipping to the lower 48 states.

    Legacy Dodge Power Wagon Trucks

    The Dodge Power Wagon entered production in 1945 as a slightly more civilized version of the WC-series military trucks made during WWII. Known internally as the T137, the Power Wagon was the first mass-produced medium-duty truck and gained a reputation for toughness and reliability. Legacy Classic Trucks is building a line of completely-restored Power Wagons that rival the original in terms of capability — and creature comforts. Starting with a western-sourced donor, Legacy completely tears down each Power Wagon and adds a modern LS V8 or diesel engine and new transmission. Marine-grade epoxy paint protects the strengthened chassis, and all-new driveline components are added. A new interior with stereo and air conditioning make each Power Wagon a great place to spend time, and a host of options are available — including a 6x6 conversion.

    Harley-Davidson LiveWire Electric Motorcycle

    With a muscular shape and aggressive stance, the Harley-Davidson LiveWire is unlike anything that's come before. The LiveWire is the latest example of the innovative spirit of the most iconic motorcycle brand in the world. Its permanent magnetic electric motor provides instant torque, so a flick of the wrist takes you from 0 to 60 in 3.0 seconds without any of the shifting or clutch work required by its combustion-based cousins. The motor sits below the battery to lower the center of gravity for easier handling, a fully adjustable Showa suspension helps improve the ride further, ABS and Traction Control for enhanced cornering and Brembo brakes ensure you can get it stopped in a hurry. The bike also has a 4.3 inch, full-color touchscreen. Built-in LTE connectivity and the H-D Connect service gives you remote access to bike vitals, its location on a map, and security alerts if it's bumped or moved through the Harley-Davidson app. Available in three color options, the LiveWire is coming to dealerships this August.

    1992 Toyota Bandeirante OJ55 Truck

    The J40 was the last of the original Land Cruisers, made from 1960 to 1984. Eight years later, Toyota was still making the J40, called the OJ55, for the Brazilian military, which is how the story of this 1992 J40 came to be in Alabama. A rare four-door soft top, the truck was completely restored in Brazil before being shipped to the States in late 2018. The most notable work done was an engine swap to a 3.8-liter Mercedes diesel four-cylinder engine, backed by a four-speed manual and two-speed transfer case — a marriage of the best of Germany and Japan. A recent repaint in Highway Green Pearl and tan canvas soft top and doors pay homage to this Land Cruiser's military origins, with grey steel wheels and BF Goodrich A/T tires. Mechanically sound and ready to drive, this rare Land Cruiser is ready for action.

    Bid Now / $22,250

    2020 Land Rover Defender SUV

    Britain might be Land Rover's birthplace, but the African continent has been its home away from home. With the new Defender getting ready to make its showroom debut, Land Rover made sure the SUV's legendary reputation was intact. This meant 750,000 miles of testing in the harshest climates around the world, and a stint helping African wildlife conservancy group Tusk in the field. Land Rover and Tusk have been partner's for 15 years, and the new Defender pulled duty at the Borana Conservancy in Kenya, helping the group with their lion conservation program. With wild lion's numbering around 20,000 worldwide, the conservation movement has never been more critical — and the Defender was there to help.

    1971 Toyota Land Cruiser G43 SUV

    Built from 1960 until 1984, the FJ43 Land Cruiser was an extremely rare model, available as a two-door medium wheelbase with either a soft or hardtop. The FJ Company managed to find one for a client and restored it into the olive green beauty pictured here. Starting with a complete teardown to the bare sheet metal, the FJ Company brought this FJ43 back to life with a caramel-colored leather interior and rear bench seats, modern electronics and amenities, and a new 4-liter Toyota V6. A subtle suspension lift makes room for new wheels and BFGoodrich tires, and a heavy-duty front bumper has a Warn winch installed. As functional as it is good looking, this FJ43 is ready for what the client needed — a stunning summer ride.
