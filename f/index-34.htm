Zelos Horizons GMT Watch

The newest addition to the Zelos family is a sleek GMT timepiece that's ready for the boardroom as well your adventurous travel agenda, and everything in between. The watch features a bronze case that will show beautiful wear over time, with an exhibition caseback that exposes the Swiss Made ETA 2893 Automatic Movement with a custom engraved rose gold plated rotor. T'The dial is made from a million year old meteorite with hands and indices covered with thick luminous C3 strips and a bright orange GMT hand and bezel markings for maximum visibility in low light conditions. It's all protected by hard sapphire crystal, and water resistant up to 200 meters, meaning that despite its handsome looks, the timepiece is designed to stand up to quite a bit of abuse. Each Horizons watch is finished with a Horween leather strap and a tropic rubber strap.

Presented by Zelos.

    Audemars Piguet Royal Oak Perpetual Calendar Ultra-Thin Watch

    It's funny that the world's thinnest perpetual calendar might have the longest name. Based on Audemars Piguet's RD#2 concept, the 41mm, titanium and platinum case of the Royal Oak Perpetual Calendar Ultra-Thin is just 6.3mm thick. The Calibre 5133 movement that powers it is just 2.89mm thick, so as to make room for the satin blue dial with white gold markers, lumed white gold hands, and the various sub-dials indicating the day, date, astronomical moon, month, leap year, and day/night. It's finished with a matching titanium bracelet and is available exclusively at Audemars Piguet boutiques.

    Zenith El Primero A384 Revival Watch

    Created to celebrate the 50th anniversary of the El Primero — the movement many consider to be the world's first automatic chronograph — Zenith is releasing the final installment of three special timepieces. Aptly named the A384 Revival after the groundbreaking watch, the new model was created using a reverse engineering approach where the original was digitized via laser and scanned to produce the most accurate 3D concept. In addition to the 37mm matte-finished steel case and lacquered white and black tachymeter dial, the Revival adds upgraded protection in the form of sapphire crystal, an exhibition caseback, and the brand new El Primero 400 movement with a 50-hour power reserve. The A384 will be available this fall with either a black leather strap with rubber lining or with an integrated metal ladder bracelet option — another homage to the original.

    Learn More From Zenith / $7,600

    Timex American Documents Watch

    One of America's signature brands dives back into domestic production with this made in the U.S.A. timepiece. Inspired by the people, culture, and landscape of the country, each American Documents watch is made by American craftsmen with American materials and a high-quality Swiss movement. The watch features a case forged from stainless steel and hand finished to a brushed satin with a polished top ring. The caseback has a brass insert that depicts the continental U.S., while Alaska and Hawaii are stamped on the black leather strap. Impact-resistant Gorilla Glass protects the minimalist white dial, a seconds sub-register, date window, and gold-plated Swiss movement. Each American Documents watch arrives in a solid cherry wood case with an inlaid magnetic closure and brass hinges that secure a felt-lined custom setting.
