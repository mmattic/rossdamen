Brad Pitt & Jennifer Aniston's Beverly Hills Estate

Brad Pitt and Jennifer Aniston were married in 2000 and the following year they moved into a 1934 Wallace Neff-designed estate in Beverly Hills. During their five years in the home, the couple respectfully renovated the interior with modern conveniences including a gourmet kitchen and digital projector room while still maintaining its historic integrity. The main house features four ensuite bedrooms, a 20-person dining room, bar, and exercise room. Its lower level opens out to an expansive stone terrace with a swimming pool and an outdoor lounge. A detached guest house offers an additional two-stories of living space, all overlooking the newly built tennis pavilion.

Photos: Hilton & Hyland

Buy From H&H / $49,000,000

    Columbia Island

    Located just off the coast of Manhattan in the Long Island Sound, Columbia Island is a remote retreat just 30 minutes from the city. The private island comes with a 5,625-square-foot residence with four bedrooms and two bathrooms. Its industrial-style interior features a spacious living area and kitchen with polished concrete floors and exposed brick walls. Expansive glazing and multiple terraces offer exceptional views while the rooftop deck affords sweeping panoramas of the New York skyline and nearby New Rochelle. Powered by solar panels and a desalination machine, the entire property is completely self-sustaining. Also included in the sale is the 3-acre Pea Island.

    Central Park Tower

    Located along New York's Billionaires' Row, the Central Park Tower is bringing 179 new condos to the neighborhood. The 1,550-foot skyscraper will be New York's largest residential building once it's completed in 2020 but has already begun putting units on the market. Starting on the 32nd floor, the listed homes range from 2,114 to 7,074 square feet and contain anywhere from 2 to 5 bedrooms, with the largest spanning across the entire 112th level. Interior's will feature generous living areas with custom details, natural finishes, and floor-to-ceiling windows affording expansive views of the city and the historic park. Ownership also comes with an impressive list of amenities that includes a 50,000-square-foot private club complete with a private fitness center, wellness spa, and an outdoor terrace with a 60-foot swimming pool, sundeck, and cabanas. Along with the residential offerings, the building will also house Nordstrom's new 7-story flagship store.

    Photos: Central Park Tower

    Buy From Central Park Tower / $6.9 - $63 Million

    TAG Heuer Autavia Isograph Watch

    Born in 1933 as an instrument for AUTOmobiles and AIRplanes, TAG Heuer has evolved their now iconic dashboard timer once again into the Autavia Isograph Watch. The timepiece nods to the 1960s model with a rounded case, beveled lugs, and an interchangeable brown calfskin strap while an oversized crown echoes to its in-flight heritage. Although its exterior is familiar, its innovative interior is built for the modern adventurer featuring a chronometer-certified Calibre 5 automatic movement with a carbon-composite hairspring, making it immune to gravity and shock. Its 42mm case comes with a polished ceramic bezel and a smoky blue, black, or grey dial.

    Gateway Canyons Ranches & Resort

    Owned by The Discovery Channel founder John Hendricks, the Gateway Canyons Ranches & Resort is a rare find for several reasons. For starters, its land is situated among some of the most impressive landscapes in the American Southwest including Cottonwood Red Rock Canyon, the La Sal Mountain range, and the Dolores River. The property boasts a variety of lodging opportunities ranging from a 22,000-square-foot adobe-style main residence to a 72-room resort. While the location lends itself to numerous outdoor activities like hiking, fishing, and horseback riding, Hendricks expanded the list of amenities with The Gateway Auto Museum, a two-story stone library, and a copper-dome observatory to take advantage of the pristine night sky. While the estate is a scenic 55-minute drive from the closest airport, thanks to the on-site helipad you can cut the commute without missing the views of the Unaweep Canyon.

    Photos: Sotheby's International Realty

    Buy From Sotheby's / $279,000,000

A villa with three additional ensuite rooms and its own kitchen is also on the property along with enough parking for 12 cars.

    Everywhere Ayfraym Cabin

    Expanding on their transportable tiny homes, Everywhere Travel Co. has created a permanent residence. Their Ayfraym Cabin uses a construction kit model, starting with planning boxes that include everything you need — from digital plans to a hammer — to begin building a modern A-frame retreat. Inspired by the vacation homes of the 1960s, the 1,574-square-foot dwelling includes three bedrooms and two bathrooms and features vaulted ceilings, wood beams, and walk-out cedar decks. Expansive glazing frames-in your location of choice to the entire interior while lookout windows provide a unique perspective to the second-story loft.     Murphy Way Estate

    Overlooking the Pacific Ocean, the Murphy Way Estate is like living in your own Mediterranean resort. The 10,000-square-foot stucco and stone dwelling is situated on a 14-acre hillside, dotted with imported olive trees. Its six-bedroom continues the modern-infused rustic aesthetic with exposed sandstone walls, walnut floors, and 300-year-old salvaged wood beams. Coastal views are always on display throughout the home's expansive glazing. You don't have to go far to find your zen. The main living area expands out to both a meditation garden with a reflecting pool and an infinity swimming pool, while the lower level offers a steam room, infrared sauna, and yoga terrace. There's also a theater, wine cellar, gym, and a three-car auto gallery to round out the impressive list of amenities.

    Photos: The Agency
